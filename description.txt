<div class="user-html user-html__with-lazy-load"><h3 id="item-description__burgerlab-burger-and-restaurant-html-template"><strong>BurgerLab – Burger &amp; Restaurant HTML Template</strong></h3>
<p><strong>BurgerLab</strong> is a clean, premium and modern HTML5 &amp; CSS3 template for burger joints, fast-food brands, cafés and modern restaurants. It ships with <strong>6 distinct homepages</strong> — Cinematic, Slider, App, Split, Statement and Bento — powered by GSAP + ScrollTrigger, plus <strong>10 header layouts</strong>, <strong>10 footer layouts</strong> and <strong>20 inner pages</strong>, all built on a single shared design-token system.
<br><br>Looks great on all screen sizes and devices. Fully responsive, fast loading, and easy to customize without deep coding knowledge.</p>
