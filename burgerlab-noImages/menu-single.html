What's inside

Built layer by layer

01

The Patty

80/20 chuck-brisket blend, smash-pressed to order on a cast-iron flat-top at 450°F for maximum crust.

02

The Cheese

Aged cheddar added on the last 30 seconds of cook — it melts into the crust, not over it.

03

The Sauce

Our house sauce: a proprietary blend we've refined across 47 iterations — we're not telling you what's in it.

04

The Bun

Amara bakes the brioche from scratch each morning — light enough to not compete, sturdy enough to hold the build.
