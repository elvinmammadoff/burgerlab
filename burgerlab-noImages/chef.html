Small kitchen, big standards. Every patty, bun and sauce that leaves the pass is signed off by one of these four — here's who's running the line tonight.

No egos, just reps

Four people, one pass

BurgerLab runs on a flat hierarchy — everyone preps, everyone grills, everyone tastes before it goes out. Between them this crew has put in two decades on flat-tops, in pastry kitchens and behind R&D test benches.

The Lab Crew

Executive chef

Marcus Reyes

Built the original Lab Burger recipe and still presses every test patty by hand. Ten years on flat-tops before that.

Grill master

Walter Briggs

Thirty years of sears behind him — sets the temperature, the timing, and the standard nobody's allowed to drop.

Pastry & bakes lead

Elena Torres

Bakes the brioche every morning before sunrise and runs dessert R&D — the lava cookie was her call.

Sauce & flavor lab

Kenji Watanabe

Guards the house sauce recipe and tastes every batch twice. Trained in fermentation labs before joining the line.
