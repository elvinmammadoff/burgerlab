The Team

Marcus Reyes

Home / Our Team / Marcus Reyes
Executive Chef

Marcus Reyes

Marcus joined BurgerLab as a junior line cook eleven years ago and worked every station in the kitchen before stepping up to Executive Chef. His early training was in classic French technique — Le Cordon Bleu, then two years in Lyon — but he found his calling the moment he first pressed a beef patty on a screaming-hot flat-top in a Manhattan pop-up kitchen.

He developed the original Lab Burger build in a two-week R&D sprint that became BurgerLab's founding menu. Today he still oversees every recipe change personally, insisting on tasting every new build at least twenty times before it reaches the pass. His philosophy is simple: great ingredients, honest heat, no shortcuts.

Outside the kitchen, Marcus teaches a bi-monthly masterclass at the restaurant and mentors young chefs through a culinary scholarship programme he co-founded in 2021. He believes the best kitchens are led by people who still love to cook — and he makes sure BurgerLab is one of them.

Ask him what he eats on his day off and he'll say a double smash, medium rare, eaten standing at the pass — just like the first one he ever made.
