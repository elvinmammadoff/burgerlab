Privacy policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BurgerLab ("we", "us") collects, uses and protects the information you share when you visit our website, place an order or book a table. This is sample template content — replace it with your own policy before going live.

1. Information we collect

When you contact us or make a reservation, we may collect your name, email address, phone number and the details of your request. We also collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited and device type to improve the site.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and confirm reservations.
  • To process and deliver orders.
  • To improve our website, menu and service.
  • To send updates only when you've opted in.

3. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and measure traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

4. Sharing your information

We never sell your personal information. We only share it with trusted service providers — such as payment and delivery partners — strictly to fulfil your request, and only as required by law.

5. Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above and protect it with appropriate technical and organisational measures.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@burgerlab.com.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hello@burgerlab.com or +1 (212) 555 — 0148.
