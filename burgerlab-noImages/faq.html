Ordering & delivery

Getting your order to you

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver every day from 11:00 to 22:30 (until 00:30 on Fridays and Saturdays). Orders placed after the cut-off are queued for the next opening.

Which areas do you deliver to?

We cover most of Manhattan within a 5 km radius of our SoHo kitchen. Enter your address at checkout and we'll confirm whether you're in range before you pay.

Can I track my order?

Yes — once your order is placed you'll get a text with a live link showing prep, cook and rider status, plus an ETA that updates in real time.

Do you offer pickup?

Absolutely. Choose "Pickup" at checkout and we'll have your order packed and ready at the counter in around 15 minutes — skip the line on busy nights.

Menu & dietary

What's in the build

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes — our plant-based patty is available on any burger, and several sides and sauces are vegan by default. Look for the leaf icon on the menu or ask staff for the current list.

Is nutrition information available?

Every item on our featured build page lists calories, protein, carbs and fat. Full nutrition sheets for the whole menu are available on request at any location.

Can I customize a burger?

Swap patties, add or remove toppings, and choose your heat level — every build on the menu can be adjusted. Add notes in the cart or mention it when ordering at the counter.

How do you handle allergies?

Let us know in the order notes or when booking and our kitchen will flag allergens and take extra care with prep surfaces. Our kitchen handles gluten, dairy, soy and nuts, so we can't guarantee a fully allergen-free environment.

Reservations & events

Booking a table

How far ahead should I book?

For standard tables, a few hours' notice is usually enough — but weekends fill fast, so booking 1–2 days ahead is safer. Groups of 8 or more should book at least 3 days out.

Can BurgerLab cater private events?

Yes — from full restaurant buyouts to office lunch drops and food truck bookings. See our services for the full list and request a quote with your headcount and date.

Is there a minimum spend or dress code?

No dress code — come as you are. Private buyouts carry a minimum spend that scales with party size and time slot; this is confirmed when you request a quote.

Payments & policies

The fine print

What payment methods do you accept?

Card, cash on delivery, Apple Pay and Google Pay for online orders; cards and cash in-store. Gift cards and vouchers can be used both online and at the counter.

What's your cancellation policy?

Orders can be changed or cancelled free of charge until the kitchen starts preparing them — usually within a few minutes of placing the order. Reservations can be cancelled up to 2 hours ahead with no charge.

How do I apply a promo code?

Enter your code in the promo field on the cart page before checkout. Discounts apply to food items only and can't be combined with other offers.

Still have questions?

We're happy to help.

Drop us a line and a real person from the team will get back to you — usually within a few hours.
