From a back-street grill to the city's favorite burger

We press every patty by hand, sear it hard on a flat-top, and stack it on brioche we toast to order. No shortcuts, no freezer — just fresh beef, sharp cheddar and a sauce we still won't share the recipe for.

Nine locations later, the rule hasn't changed: every burger leaves the pass in under 30 minutes, built exactly to spec.
