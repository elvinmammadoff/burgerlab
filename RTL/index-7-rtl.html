Our story

Obsessed with the perfect bite since day one.

BurgerLab started as a single flat-top in SoHo. We set one rule: every patty gets hand-pressed, every bun gets toasted, no shortcuts. Eight years and nine locations later, the rule hasn't changed.

We source our beef blend fresh every morning, cut the brioche buns in-house and blend the house sauce in small weekly batches. The quality isn't marketing — it's what we do at 6am before the first order drops.

The lab crew

Meet the people behind the food

Three chefs, one obsession — the perfect burger. They press, season and grill every build before the first order hits the board.

Executive chef

Marcus Reyes

Built the original Lab Burger recipe and still presses every test patty by hand. Ten years on flat-tops before that.

Grill master

Walter Briggs

Thirty years of sears behind him — sets the temperature, the timing, and the standard nobody's allowed to drop.

Pastry & bakes lead

Elena Torres

Bakes the brioche every morning before sunrise and runs dessert R&D — the lava cookie was her call.

Sauce & flavor lab

Kenji Watanabe

Guards the house sauce recipe and tastes every batch twice. Trained in fermentation labs before joining the line.
